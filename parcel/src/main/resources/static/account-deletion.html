YouDash Express - Account Deletion

Users can delete their account using the app or request deletion manually.

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  • Open the app
  • Go to Profile
  • Click "Delete Account"

Option 2: Request via Email

  • Email: info@youdashexpress.com
  • Subject: Delete My Account
  • Include your registered phone number

Data Deleted

  • Profile information
  • Addresses
  • Order history

Data Retained

  • Transaction records (for legal purposes)

Deletion Timeline

Deletion requests are processed within 3-5 working days.

For any issue, contact info@youdashexpress.com.
